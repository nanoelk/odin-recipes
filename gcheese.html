A grilled cheese is a toasted sandwich filled with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cheese of your choice.
  • Bread of your choice.
  • Butter.

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan on medium and melt butter in pan.
  2. Sandwich the cheese between 2 slices of bread.
  3. Toast the sandwich the in butter until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
